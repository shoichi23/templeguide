Main Page

Karasuyama Teramachi is located in the Northern Karasuyama from 2nd block through 6th block. Around Teramachi Street, there are as many as 26 temples, each of them has original history. Most of them are moved from different places because of the urbanization of Tokyo or escaping from the Great Kanto Earthquake in 1923.

Even though it is in Kita Karasuyama, Setagaya city, the street reminds the visitors of Kyoto city. Thus, this area is well known as little Kyoto, meaning a city that resembles Kyoto in their old streets, buildings and atmosphere

Also, this street is one of the best 100 beautiful spots selected by the citizens of Setagaya city that aimed to make people interested in this city established in 1984.

This street is covered in silence so visitors enjoy their small journey in the Karasuyama Teramachi.

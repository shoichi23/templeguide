Kogen-in Temple

Kogen-in is dedicated to Amitabha Tathagata. Kogen-in temple (officially Zuisen-san Kogen-in) was built by Arima Yorimoto, the fourth lord of the Kurume domain in 1702. Kogen-in was originally located in Shinagawa but gutted by fire due to the Great Kanto Earthquake in 1923. In 1935, this temple was rebuilt and moved to Karasuyama.
